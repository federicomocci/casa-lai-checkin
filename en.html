Check-in step by step
1

Arrive at the gate

The address is Via Capo Falcone 11, Stintino. Stop the car in front of the main gate.

One person gets out and walks in through the small pedestrian gate on the left (it is always open).

2

Get the keys

Once inside the courtyard, walk straight ahead toward the right until you reach the wooden notice board.

On the left side of the board you will find a small black key safe.

🔢 Key safe code We sent it to you on WhatsApp together with this link.

Inside you'll find the house key set.

⚠️ Before walking away Close the safe and scramble the combination wheels, so it stays secure for the next guests.
3

Open the vehicle gate

Go back to the car. Right next to the main gate, in the stone wall, there is a lock.

The gate opens automatically. Drive in, and it closes by itself after a few seconds.

🅿️ Parking Once inside, you can park anywhere in the courtyard.
4

Walk down to the house

From the wooden board, head left toward the first steps going down.

Go down, turn left, then keep walking down, staying on the right. The path winds between the stone houses with sea views.

✅ Don't worry You can't get lost. The path is one way only: just keep walking down until you arrive in front of the house.

At the end you reach the final stairs leading to unit no. 8.

5

Open the front door

The entrance is down to the left, under the bamboo pergola.

You will recognise the door by the "Fam. Lai" plaque and the seahorse-shaped handle 🐎

Open with the large key from the keychain. Welcome! 🎉

🆘 Lost your keys?

Don't worry. On the wooden post in front of the door there is a second white key safe with a backup set of keys inside. The code is the same as the first one (the one we sent you on WhatsApp).
